Biography

Haochen Wang is currently a first year Ph.D. student at the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing, National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Zhaoxiang Zhang. Before that, he got his Bachelor's degree in from the School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University at June 2022.

His research focuses on computer vision and pattern recognition, particularly on the following topics:

  • Image Perception
  • Label-Efficient Learning
  • Unsupervised Representation Learning
